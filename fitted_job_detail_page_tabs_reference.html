Salary negotiation
The negotiation starts before they make an offer. Exact scripts for Reformation.
When they ask your expectations
"I'm open based on the full picture. Could you share the budgeted range?"
Don't name a number first. Whoever speaks first anchors low.
When they give you a number
"Based on my experience, I was thinking closer to [top third + 10%]. Is there flexibility?"
For $46–56k, aim for the high end. Never accept on the spot.
If the range is firm
"Is there flexibility on start date, title, remote days, or a 90-day review?"
Salary isn't the only lever. PTO, title, and reviews are negotiable too.
Never say
"I really need this job" or "I'll take whatever you're offering."
Even if true, it transfers all the power.
