III. On Returning A short chapter

It learns. It waits.
Then it checks in.

fitted. isn't a one-time tool. It grows with your career. Come back after a new job, a promotion, or a pivot — and it already knows your history.

A two-minute conversation recalibrates your matches, refreshes your profile, and resets your guidance for where you are now.

You tell fitted. who you are
Answer a few questions about your background, goals, and what matters to you. fitted. builds your profile from the start — no résumé upload required.
fitted. tracks your progress
Every application, saved role, and piece of feedback trains your profile. fitted. gets sharper the more you use it.
Your goals evolve — so does fitted.
Got a promotion? Pivoting industries? A short check-in recalibrates everything — your matches, your résumé targets, your salary data.
Always in your corner
Whether you're actively searching or just keeping options open, fitted. stays ready. Come back any time — it picks up right where you left off.
IV. On Your Device Add to home screen

Add fitted. to your home screen.

fitted. lives on your phone, not in a browser tab. Installed, it opens like an app — fast, full-screen, and there when you need it.

iPhone & iPad
Safari only
  1. Open fitted. in Safari
    Chrome on iOS doesn't support installation — use Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button
    A box with an arrow pointing upward, at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen
    Scroll down in the share sheet if you don't see it.
  4. Tap Add
    fitted. now opens like any other app — full-screen, no browser chrome.

Android & Chrome
Chrome · Edge · Brave
  1. Open fitted. in Chrome
    Works the same in Edge and Brave on Android or desktop.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu
    Top-right corner. On desktop, look for an install icon in the address bar.
  3. Choose Install app
    Or Add to Home Screen — wording varies slightly by browser.
  4. Confirm Install
    fitted. opens in its own window — like a native app, separate from your browser.
Why install?
Faster load. Offline access to your saved roles and notes. Gentle nudges when a strong match appears — nothing more.
