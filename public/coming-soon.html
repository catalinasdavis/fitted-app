What is fitted.

Not a job board.
A guide that knows you.

fitted. reads your background, learns your preferences, and scores every real job opening against who you actually are — not just your keywords. It tells you where you stand, what's working, and what you need to hear, even if no one else will say it.

When you come back after time away, fitted. checks in. It sends a quick quiz to see how things have changed — a new job search, a pivot, a promotion. Then it recalibrates everything to match where you are now.

fitted. remembers you

It learns. It waits.
Then it checks in.

"You've been gone a while. Things change. Let's see where you are now — it takes two minutes."

fitted. isn't a one-time tool. It grows with your career. Come back after a new job, a promotion, or a pivot — and it already knows your history. A short check-in quiz recalibrates your matches, refreshes your profile, and resets your guidance for where you are now.

First visit
You tell fitted. who you are. It builds your profile and starts scoring matches immediately.
Active search
Matches update daily. fitted. tracks your applications, sends nudges, and gives candid guidance as you progress.
You land a role. You disappear.
fitted. notices. It holds your history and waits — no spam, no nudges while you're settled.
You return — it's ready
A two-minute quiz re-calibrates your profile. New title, new seniority, new goals — fitted. updates everything and resumes where you left off.
Add to your home screen

fitted. lives on your phone,
not in a browser tab.

Install it to your home screen and it works like an app — fast, offline-ready, always there when you need it.

iPhone / iPad
Safari browser
1
Open fitted.app in Safari
Must use Safari — Chrome on iOS doesn't support install.
2
Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
It looks like a box with an arrow pointing upward (↑).
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
You'll see the fitted. icon and name — tap Add.
4
fitted. now appears on your home screen
Open it just like any app — full screen, no browser chrome.

Chrome, Edge, or Brave
1

2

4
Click Install in the prompt
fitted. opens in its own window, separate from your browser — like a native app.

Faster load times, offline access to your saved roles and notes, and native notifications when new high-match jobs appear.
